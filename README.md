# learning-toolkit

Research and strategy artefacts for the Early Years Toolkit, an early-childhood-education product Clare is building. This folder is the advisory and research workspace that feeds Clare's product decisions, not the product codebase itself.

## What's here

- `_docs/` strategic research, market analysis, gap analyses, retro notes, and the all-in-one project pack
- `_docs/source-materials/` Clare's source artefacts: pitch deck, presentation, planning sheets, audio interviews + transcripts, and the transcribe.py script

## Roles

- **Clare**, founder of the Early Years Toolkit
- **Chrissy**, advisor and sounding board (not the founder, not building, not the public face)

## Working in this folder

- Voice: AU English, plain language, no marketing jargon
- Naming: lowercase kebab-case (audio files and their transcripts keep their machine-generated names)
- Audio files (`*.mp4`) and the private notes file (`clare-feedback-notes.md`) are gitignored and live locally only
